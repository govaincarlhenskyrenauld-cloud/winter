Like You're 5

We live here

It's a big sphere-shaped rock called Earth, and it spins

And Earch orbits around this

It's also a sphere, but not exactly a rock. More like an *extremely hot* ball of lava and hot gas. It's called the Sun and it's hundreds of times bigger than Earth

Like this:

If Earth was perfectly vertical, both emispheres would get as much sunlight and heat from the sun.
But the thing is:

IT's TILTED!

And because of the tilt, one emisphere is always closer to the Sun than the other.
The closer emisphere gets more sunlight at better angles (closer to 90 degrees), therefore more heat.
And the other gets less sunlight at worse angles, therefore less heat.

The emisphere that's further from the sun gets cold, because much less of its territory is hit by the sun's light and heat, and the part that's hit gets much less heat because of the bad sunlight angles, which spread the heat and light more.

AND THAT'S HOW WINTER WORKS!
